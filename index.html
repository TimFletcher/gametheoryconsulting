Game Theory Consulting

The goal of Game Theory Consulting is to be a vendor and product agnostic sales organization.

Contrary to what some IT solutions providers will tell you, nobody is an expert at everything. Every organization wants to improve and solve either internal or external issues but unfortunately they usually don't know that a solution exists that solves their problem or they do not have the time to do the research to find the best solution for their organization.

Most business will pick the solutions that they last heard about or the one referred to them this usually results in a partial solutions and the customer being somewhat dissatisfied. Game Theory Consulting sales approach is to learn about our clients discover what is their biggest organizational issue is that they need solved and the find the people or vendor to solve that problem.

Some organizations may not have a clear picture of their biggest issue in such cases we can bring in either a management consultant or a business analyst to help them identify their issues. Once we have identified the client issue or need we will work with our vendor network to identify the best possible solutions to meet that client's needs. When we have narrowed down possible solutions each vendor will make a proposal and a presentation to the client we will assist the client in the decision making process by acting as a neutral third party advisor.
